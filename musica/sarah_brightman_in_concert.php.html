SARAH BRIGHTMAN IN CONCERT

"Sarah Brightman in Concert"
(1998 - 75m)

SINOPSE
Sarah Brightman
In Concert at the Royal Albert Hall
With the English National Orchestra conducted by Paul Bateman and special guests Andrea Bocelli & Andrew Lloyd Webber

SARAH BRIGHTMAN performs a selection of timeless classics with the English National Orchestra at London's Royal Albert Hall, where she was filmed in concert in Septembter 1997 for her first solo release on video. The concert includes a live performance of the international hit duet "Time To Say Goodbye" with ANDREA BOCELLI, and a guest appearance by ANDREW LLOYD WEBBER on "Whistle Down The Wind".
