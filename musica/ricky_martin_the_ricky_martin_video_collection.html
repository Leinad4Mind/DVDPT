RICKY MARTIN: THE RICKY MARTIN VIDEO COLLECTION

"Ricky Martin: The Ricky Martin Video Collection" (1999 - 40m)

SINOPSE
More than a billion people across the globe felt the power and energy of Ricky Martin's electrifying performance of The Cup Of Life (La Copa De La Vida) at the 1999 Grammy Awards ceremony. That same night, Ricky received the Best Latin Pop Performance for his Vuelve album.
When Ricky Martin, his self-titled English-language debut album, entered the charts at No. 1 in May 1999, Ricky already had the No. 1 single in the country, the irresistible Livin' La Vida Loca (the chart-topping, record-breaking No. 1 international megahit performed in English and in Spanish).
This collection includes the Grammy performance of The Cup Of Life, She's All I Ever Had (in English and Spanish), and Spanish-language performances of Vuelve and La Bomba.
The DVD also features an exclusive interview and behind-the-scenes footage of Ricky on stage and in the studio. The arrival of Ricky Martin marks a new era in pop music, a time when the whole world is Livin' La Vida Loca!
